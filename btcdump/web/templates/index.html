RSI ? --
Relative Strength Index (14). Measures momentum. <30 = oversold (buy signal), >70 = overbought (sell signal). Click to verify on TradingView.
MACD ? --
Moving Average Convergence Divergence (12,26,9). Bull = MACD above signal line (upward momentum). Bear = below (downward). Click to verify.
Stoch ? --
Stochastic %K (14,3). Oscillator 0-100. <20 = oversold, >80 = overbought. Measures where price closed relative to range. Click to verify.
ADX ? --
Average Directional Index (14). Measures trend strength. <20 = no trend, 20-40 = developing, >40 = strong trend. Does NOT show direction. Click to verify.
ATR ? --
Average True Range (14). Measures volatility in $. Higher = more volatile price swings. Used for stop-loss placement and R/R calculation. Click to verify.
